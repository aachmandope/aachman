TAIL TRAILS

This collection of pet-related bags explores the intersection of functionality, comfort, and thoughtful design. Each bag is developed with attention to usability, material choice, and the needs of both pets and users, balancing practicality with refined aesthetics. This collection reflects my exploration of pet-centric product design, addressing real-world requirements such as comfort, mobility, and user interaction. The designs demonstrate a problem-solving approach grounded in functionality and material understanding.

THE LEDGER ARTEFACT

This collection explores the use of hair-on leather to create tactile, visually distinctive surfaces. Consisting of a tote and two clutch bags, the designs balance structured form with material expression, highlighting craftsmanship, durability, and refined detailing.

The collection features a tote and two clutch bags developed using hair-on leather, emphasizing texture, contrast, and material character. Tooling techniques enhance the surface while maintaining functional, well-defined silhouettes. This collection reinterprets tooling bags through material experimentation with hair-on leather. The tote and clutches combine structured construction with organic texture, creating a dialogue between rugged utility and refined design

CONCLUSION

These projects represent an ongoing exploration of form, material, and user experience. Through experimentation and refinement, the work demonstrates my interest in translating ideas into well-resolved, meaningful design outcomes.
